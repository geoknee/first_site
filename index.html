George C. Knee

George is a quantum information theorist. He has a Bsc. in Physics and Philosophy from King's College London, an Msc. in Theoretical Physics from Imperial College London, and recently earned his doctorate from Oxford University, U.K. He is interested in the foundations of physics and its technological application. He has worked on experimental tests of the superposition principle and theoretical analyses of quantum metrology. He has an ongoing interest in quantum computing, and now works at NTT Basic Research Laboratories, Japan.
